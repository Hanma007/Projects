A Guardian’s Guide to
Discord

Discord’s Guardian’s Guide is a centralized asset that helps adults who
are raising teens understand the latest highlights of how Discord works,
information on how their teen might spend time on Discord, and shares
tips and resources to talk to teens about keeping their online
experiences comfortable.
